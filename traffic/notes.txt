snapped_road_segments.csv was generated by shooting "rays" towards the terrain surface.

The snapped_roads.geojson can be open in paraview


The Pollutant emission were estimated based on:
* The Portuguese average car fleet https://apambiente.pt/sites/default/files/_Clima/Inventarios/20250509/iir_2025_v2.pdf (Table C-8)
* The emission factors by car type from: https://www.eea.europa.eu/en/analysis/publications/emep-eea-guidebook-2023/part-b-sectoral-guidance-chapters/1-energy/1-a-combustion/1-a-3-b-i-1/@@download/file
	* And computed through Equation 25 of https://www.eea.europa.eu/en/analysis/publications/emep-eea-guidebook-2023/part-b-sectoral-guidance-chapters/1-energy/1-a-combustion/1-a-3-b-i/@@download/file

* The time distribution was assumed through Master 8 from the Municipal Noise Map: https://www.cm-guimaraes.pt/cmguimaraes/uploads/document/file/13433/Mapa_de_Ruido_Guimaraes_Relatorio.pdf (Document is in Portuguese->sorry)
